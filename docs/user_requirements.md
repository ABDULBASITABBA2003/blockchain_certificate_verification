# User Requirements

## 1. Student / Guest (Public User)
- The Student/Guest shall be able to view the home page.
- The Student/Guest shall be able to navigate to the "Verify Certificate" page.
- The Student/Guest shall be able to verify a certificate by entering a registration number or certificate hash.
- The Student/Guest shall be able to view the verification result, including student details and certificate validity status.
- The Student/Guest shall be able to view the "About" page to learn more about the system.
- The Student/Guest shall be able to view the "Contact" page to see contact information.

## 2. School Admin
- The School Admin shall be able to log in to the system using their email and password.
- The School Admin shall be able to view a dashboard displaying a list of uploaded certificates for their institution.
- The School Admin shall be able to search for certificates by student name or registration number within their institution.
- The School Admin shall be able to upload a new certificate, providing student details (Name, Reg No, Department, Faculty, Program, Level, Graduation Year) and uploading the certificate file and passport photo.
- The School Admin shall be able to edit the details of an existing certificate uploaded by their institution.
- The School Admin shall be able to delete a certificate uploaded by their institution.
- The School Admin shall be able to log out of the system.
- The School Admin shall be restricted from accessing or modifying certificates from other institutions.

## 3. Super Admin
- The Super Admin shall be able to log in to the system using their email and password.
- The Super Admin shall be able to view a dashboard displaying a list of all registered School Admins.
- The Super Admin shall be able to search for School Admins by name, email, or school name.
- The Super Admin shall be able to add a new School Admin, providing their name, email, password, and associated school name.
- The Super Admin shall be able to view the details of a specific School Admin.
- The Super Admin shall be able to edit the details (Name, Email, School Name, Password) of an existing School Admin.
- The Super Admin shall be able to delete a School Admin account.
- The Super Admin shall be able to log out of the system.
